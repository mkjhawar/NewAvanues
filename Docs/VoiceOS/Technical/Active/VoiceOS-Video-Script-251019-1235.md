## PRODUCTION NOTES

### GENERAL GUIDELINES:

**Visual Style:**
- Modern, clean, tech-forward
- Use actual UI/apps, not mockups
- Show real people in diverse settings
- Professional color grading (teal/orange cinematic look)

**Audio:**
- Professional voiceover (warm, confident, clear)
- Background music (upbeat, modern, not overpowering)
- Sound effects for UI interactions
- Voiceover volume: -12dB
- Music volume: -24dB (ducking under voiceover)

**Text Overlays:**
- Montserrat Bold for headlines
- Open Sans for body text
- Animations: Smooth fade-ins (200ms)
- Never more than 10 words on screen
- High contrast (white text on dark, or vice versa)

**Pacing:**
- Quick cuts for demos (2-3s per shot)
- Longer shots for explanations (5-8s)
- Match voiceover rhythm
- Leave 1s breathing room between sections

### VIDEO DELIVERABLES:

**Formats:**
- 16:9 (YouTube, Website): 1920x1080
- 9:16 (Instagram, TikTok): 1080x1920
- 1:1 (Social Square): 1080x1080

**Versions:**
- Hero 90s (with voiceover)
- Hero 30s (cut-down version)
- Hero 15s (teaser)
- Feature Spotlights (60s each)
- Social cuts (15s, no voiceover, subtitles only)

**Export Settings:**
- H.264 codec
- 8Mbps bitrate (1080p)
- AAC audio, 192kbps
- Include subtitle SRT files
