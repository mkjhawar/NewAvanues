## Welcome to VoiceOS!

VoiceOS is a voice-first accessibility platform that lets you control any Android app with your voice, even if the app wasn't designed for voice control. This manual will help you get started and master all of VoiceOS's features.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why is exploration faster in the new version?

**Answer:** The December 2025 update includes major performance optimizations:

| Improvement | Technical Detail | User Impact |
|-------------|------------------|-------------|
| **Batch Database Operations** | Reduced from 315 DB ops ‚Üí 2 ops per screen (157x reduction) | 27x faster element registration |
| **Click-Before-Register** | Elements clicked while fresh (not stale) | 95%+ elements now clickable (was 30-50%) |
| **Memory Optimization** | Fixed coroutine leaks in accessibility service | No crashes during long exploration sessions |

**Example:** A 63-element screen (like RealWear "My Controls") now takes 45ms to process instead of 1351ms.

**Bottom line:** You can explore entire apps in seconds instead of minutes, and VoiceOS captures nearly every element instead of missing half of them.
